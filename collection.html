The Belgian Postcards Collection

In 2017, the KU Leuven University Library launched an ambitious digitization project focusing on a large collection of Belgian travel postcards dating from the late 19th to the 20th century. The postcards come from a private collection of more than 100,000 items, generously donated to the University by the family of the original collector after his passing. So far the University Library has digitized 75.000 postcards, which can be consulted online on the KU Leuven University Library website.
